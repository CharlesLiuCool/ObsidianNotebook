### **Example**

$S \to 0S1|01$

We do
$S \to 0S1$
${\;\;\to 00S11}$
$\;\;\to00011$

___

First three steps always same
$\delta (q_0, \Lambda, Z_0) = \{(q, SZ_0)\}$
$\delta (q, \Lambda, Z_0) = \{(q_1, Z_0)\}$
$\delta (q, a, a) = \{(q, \Lambda)\}, a \in \Sigma$

Last step is just (for terminal symbols, e.g. $A$, and products $\alpha$ of $A$)
$\delta(q, \Lambda, A) = \{(q,\alpha)\}$
