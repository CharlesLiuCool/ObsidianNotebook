8. Give a context-free grammar that generates language $$\{0^n1^m : n \neq 2m\}$$

This is a union of $\{0^n1^m : n > 2m\}$ and $\{0^n1^m : n < 2m\}$

$S \to A|B$

$A$ here is $\{0^n1^m : n > 2m\}$ 
$A \to 0A \,|\, 00A1 \,|\, 0$

$B$ here is $\{0^n1^m : n < 2m\}$
$B \to 00B1 \,|\, 0B1 \,|\, B1 |\, 01 \,|\, 1$

So our context-free grammar is:
$$\begin{align} S &\to A|B \\ A &\to 0A \,|\, 00A1 \,|\, 0 \\ B &\to 00B1 \,|\, 0B1 \,|\, B1 |\, 01 \,|\, 1 \end{align}$$

9. Eliminate Λ-productions from the following context-free grammar:  
$$S \to ASB|AB|ab$$  
$$A \to AS|a|\Lambda$$
$$B \to SB|A|b$$

When we get rid of the $\Lambda$ in $A$, we get
$\begin{align}S &\to ASB|SB|AB|B|ab \\ A &\to AS|S|a \\ B &\to SB|A|\Lambda|b \end{align}$

Removing the $\Lambda$ from $B$, we get
$\begin{align}S &\to ASB|AS|SB|S|AB|A|B|ab|\Lambda \\ A &\to AS|S|a \\ B &\to SB|S|A|b \end{align}$

Finally, removing $\Lambda$ from $S$, we get
$$\begin{align}S &\to ASB|AS|SB|S|AB|A|B|ab\\ A &\to AS|A|S|a \\ B &\to SB|S|A|b \end{align}$$

11. Transform the following grammar into CNF (Chomsky normal form):  
$$S \to AbBaS|ASB|AB|ab$$
$$A \to bB|a$$
$$B \to SBb|b$$

Step 1: Remove $\Lambda$-productions. No $\Lambda$-productions, so nothing to remove.

Step 2: Get rid of terminals

$S \to AbBaS|ASB|AB|ab$
$A \to bB|a$
$B \to SBb|b$

First define two non-terminal variables
$C \to a$
$D \to b$

We get
$S \to ADBCS|ASB|AB|CD$
$A \to DB|C$
$B \to SBD|D$
$C \to a$
$D \to b$


Step 3: Reduce productions longer than two symbols

$S \to ADBCS|ASB|AB|CD$
$A \to DB|C$
$B \to SBD|D$
$C \to a$
$D \to b$

Define
$E \to BD$
$F \to SB$
$G \to DBCS$
$H \to BCS$
$I \to CS$

We get
$S \to AG|AF|AB|CD$
$A \to DB|C$
$B \to SE|D$
$E \to BD$
$F \to SB$
$G \to DH$
$H \to BI$
$I \to CS$
$C \to a$
$D \to b$

Step 4: Remove unit productions

Remove $A \to C$ and $B \to D$
$S \to AG|AF|AB|CD$
$A \to DB|a$
$B \to SE|b$
$E \to BD$
$F \to SB$
$G \to DH$
$H \to BI$
$I \to CS$
$C \to a$
$D \to b$

So we get

$$S \to AG|AF|AB|CD$$

$$E \to BD$$
