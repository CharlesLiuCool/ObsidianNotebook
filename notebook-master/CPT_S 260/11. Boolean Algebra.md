### **Notations Being Used**

- $+$ Denotes OR operation
- $\cdot$ Denotes AND operation
- $\sim$ Denotes negation operation

### **Laws**

>*Associative Law*
$A \cdot (B \cdot C) = (A \cdot B) \cdot C$

>*Annulment Law*
$A \cdot 0 = 0$
$A + 1 = 1$

>*Identity Law*
$A \cdot 1 = A$
$A + 0 = A$

>*Complement Law*
$A + \bar{A} = 1$
$A \cdot \bar{A}$

>*Double Negation Law*
$\bar{A} = A$

>*Absorption Law*
$A \cdot (A + B) = A$
$A + AB = A$
$A + \bar{A}B = A + B$

>*Idempotent Law*
$A + A = A$
$A \cdot A = A$

>*Distributive Law of Multiplication*
>$A + BC = (A+B)(A+C)$

### **DeMorgan's Law**

>$\overline{AB} = \overline{A} + \overline{B}$
>$\overline(A+B) = \overline{A} + \overline{B}$
