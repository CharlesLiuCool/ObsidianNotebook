### **What on Earth is New Western History?**

>[!question] What makes New Western History more powerful in comparison to older models? What are some setbacks of it?

- Reject "end of the frontier" and the term "frontier" as it comes with nationalistic and ethnocentric ideas
- Instead think of it as a process and the New West as a region vaguely West of Mississippi River
- Break free from idea of "progress", instead acknowledging that there was some setbacks
- Acknowledge different point of views
