>[!question] 51
>Find the volume of the solid outside of the cone ${\phi = \dfrac{\pi}{4}}$ and inside the sphere ${\rho = 4 \cos \phi}$

>[!check]- Solution
>![[20. 2024-03-28 08.14.49.excalidraw]]
>Volume = 
>$${\int_0^{2\pi} \int_\frac{\pi}{4}^\frac{\pi}{2} \int_0^{4\cos\phi} \rho^2 \sin\phi\,d\rho d\phi d\theta}$$

>[!question] 49
>Use spherical coordinates to find the volume of the solid bounded by the sphere ${\rho = 2\cos\theta}$ and the hemisphere ${\rho = 1}$, ${z \geq 0}$

>[!check]- Solution
>![[20. 2024-03-28 08.25.54.excalidraw]]
> Volume = 
> $${2 \int_0^{2\pi} \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{3}} \int_{\frac{1}{2}\sec\phi}^1 1 \rho^2\sin\phi d\rho d\phi d\theta}$$
