We have to optimize our circuits.

- Boolean Algebra

**AND** laws:

${A \cdot 1 = A}$
${A \cdot 0 = 0}$
${A \cdot A = A}$
${A \cdot \overline A = 0}$

**OR** Laws:

${A + 1 = 1}$
${A + 0 = A}$
${A + A = A}$
${A + \overline A = 1}$

**Commutative Law**

${A \cdot B = B \cdot A}$
${A \cdot B \cdot C = B \cdot A \cdot C = C \cdot B \cdot A}$

**Associative Law**

${(A \cdot B) \cdot C = A \cdot (B \cdot C)}$

**Factoring/Distributive**

${Y <= A \cdot (B + C) = A \cdot B + A \cdot C}$
${Y <= A + B \cdot C \iff (A + B) \cdot (A + C)}$

**DeMorgan's Law**

${\overline{A \cdot B} = \overline A + \overline B}$
${\overline{A + B} = \overline A \cdot \overline B}$

**Absorptive Law**

${Y <= A + \overline A \cdot B <= A + B}$
${Y <= A \cdot (\overline A + \overline B) <= A + B}$
