### Motivation

Let ${T}$: ${\mathbb{R}^2 \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^3}$ be a linear transformation with 

$${T(e_1) = \begin{bmatrix} \,1\, \\ 1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix}, T(e_2) = \begin{bmatrix} \,1\, \\ 2 \\ 0\end{bmatrix}}$$
where ${e_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix}}$ and ${e_2 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}}$.

**Question 1:** For ${x} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ -5 \end{bmatrix}$, can we find ${T(x)}$?
