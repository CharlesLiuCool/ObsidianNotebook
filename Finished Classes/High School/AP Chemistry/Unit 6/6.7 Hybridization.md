To put it simply, the hybridization is the ${\text{sp}^{\text{\# of electron domains - 1}}}$

For example, if the atom has 3 electron domains, its hybridization is ${\text{sp}^2}$

If an atom only has 1 electron domain, its hybridization is just ${\text{s}}$
